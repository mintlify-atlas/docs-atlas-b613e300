## What is StudyQuest?

StudyQuest is an AI-powered mobile study platform that transforms PDF documents into interactive, gamified learning experiences. Built with Flutter and powered by Google Gemini AI, StudyQuest automatically generates flashcards and quizzes from your study materials, making learning more engaging and effective.

## How It Works

StudyQuest follows a simple but powerful workflow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Your PDF">
    Select any study material - textbooks, lecture notes, research papers - and upload it to StudyQuest. The app stores it securely in Supabase Storage.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="AI Processing">
    A Python worker powered by Google Gemini AI processes your document:
    - Extracts text from all pages
    - Identifies key concepts and topics
    - Generates relevant flashcards with questions and answers
    - Creates multiple-choice quizzes with explanations
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Interactive Study">
    Access your personalized study materials:
    - Review flashcards with spaced repetition
    - Take quizzes to test your knowledge
    - Track mastery levels for each concept
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Progress">
    Monitor your learning journey with gamified metrics:
    - Complete levels and topics
    - Earn XP and achievements
    - View your study streaks and statistics
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Backend Worker (Python)

A Python worker monitors Supabase for new documents and processes them:

```python
# Main worker loop
while True:
    response = supabase.table('documents').select("*").eq('status', 'processing').execute()
    documents = response.data
    
    if documents and len(documents) > 0:
        for doc in documents:
            process_document(doc)
    else:
        time.sleep(5)
```

### Database Schema

The Supabase database contains these main tables:

- **documents**: Uploaded PDF files with processing status
- **topics**: AI-generated topics extracted from documents
- **flashcards**: Question-answer pairs for each topic
- **quizzes**: Multiple-choice questions with explanations
- **user_profiles**: User progress and gamification data
